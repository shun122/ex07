Urban Systems Theory

Friday 3rd period

Cities throughout history have been spaces where societal changes are most evident. This course focuses on the phenomenon of urbanization, which began in the late 18th century and progressed until the 20th century.

It examines the formation and development processes of cities from various aspects such as politics, economy, society, and culture, thereby considering the various issues cities face.
